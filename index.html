    / Services
    What we do
    Staff Augmentation is what we do. The global staffing industry, one of the primary suppliers of talent is estimated to support $490 billion in annual spend. Staff augmentation, like any organizational strategy comes with its own list of pros and cons. It is up individual hiring and project managers to determine whether staff augmentation is the right model for their specific needs. The following table may provide some guidance:
    Increased capacity
    Increase in labor to fulfil a temporary spike in everyday work such as project work, increased production or spikes in customer demand.
    Bridge to Hire
    Used for longer term needs as a bridge to provide capacity while the company hires a permanent team in a new location or a new line of business.
    Specialized Skills
    Used for out-of-house specialized skills that are required for a period of time such as brand and logo design or technology setup.
    Strategic Hiring
    Build teams using flexible labor as a way to gain competitive advantage, increase speed to market or be more agile.
